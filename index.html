About Us

Our Vision

Working Together for Success and Independence

At Kwandabezinhle Primary School, we embrace a powerful vision: to work together. Our community—educators, learners, parents, and neighbors—joins hands to create an environment where quality education thrives. We believe that this collaborative spirit will lead our learners toward success and independence.

Our Mission

  • Teaching and Learning First: Every decision we make centers on our students. Their growth, well-being, and academic journey are our top priorities.
  • Empowering Educators: We provide our dedicated educators with opportunities to develop and enhance their skills. Their passion fuels our students’ learning experiences.
  • Preparing Responsible Adults: Beyond textbooks, we instill values, resilience, and a sense of responsibility. Our learners are not just students; they are future leaders.
  • Community Commitment: Together, we work tirelessly to garner support from our community. With their backing, we modernize and fully develop our school premises, creating a nurturing space for all.

Our Principal: Mr. AM Mbutho

Mr. Mbutho, our efficient and dedicated principal, leads by example. His unwavering commitment amplifies the impact of our small school, making it greater than its physical size.
